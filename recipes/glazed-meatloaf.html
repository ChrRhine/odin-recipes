Glazed Meatloaf

Description

This is a succulent glazed meatloaf with a sweet and sour sauce that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, divided
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 slices bread, broken up into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon beef bouillon granules

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine ketchup, brown sugar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and mustard powder in a small bowl.
  3. Combine ground beef, bread, onion, egg, bouillon, and remaining lemon juice in a separate large bowl. Add in 1/3 of the ketchup mixture from the small bowl. Mix well and place in a 5x9-inch loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Drain any excess fat, coat with remaining ketchup mixture, and bake for 10 more minutes.
